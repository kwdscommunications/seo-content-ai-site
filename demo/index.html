Demo summary

What merchants will see in the app

SEO Content AI helps Shopify merchants create, review, and apply SEO content from one workspace. Generate product and collection SEO titles, meta descriptions, descriptions, image alt text, and SEO metafields without jumping between tools. Drafts are reviewed inside the app first, then applied back to Shopify when ready.

AI-assisted generation

Generate SEO content from product and collection data, with brand settings and merchant preferences shaping the output.

Template fallback

When AI is unavailable or disabled, merchants can still use template-based workflows instead of hitting a dead end.

Embedded admin experience

The app runs inside Shopify Admin, keeping content creation close to the resources merchants are already managing.
